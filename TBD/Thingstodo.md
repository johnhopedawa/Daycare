Ideas

Dashboard – birthdays, room ratios, today’s attendance, alerts (overdue paperwork, expiring immunizations, overdue invoices)

My School – list of rooms with capacity vs enrolled, assigned staff, quick link into each room’s activities/notes

Attendance – daily check-in/out, absent/late reasons, late pickup log, quick export for licensing

Messaging – room-wide announcements, 1:1 parent messages, templates for common messages (late opening, reminders, etc.)

Billing – invoices and payments, credit/subsidy tracking, overdue list, simple revenue snapshot

Enrollment – lead list (prospects), tours scheduled/completed, waitlist, upcoming start dates and room transitions

Paperwork – required forms per child, expiring documents (immunizations, physicals), upload/track who turned in what

Admissions – child profiles with picture + name, status (prospect/enrolled/waitlist), quick links to messaging, billing, paperwork

Staff Scheduling – weekly schedule by room, time-off requests, coverage warnings when ratios are off

Marketing – track tours and referrals, source of each family (Google, friend, flyer), simple “what’s working” view

Website – basic content (hours, closures, calendar), announcements that can also push to parents

Reporting – dropdown for attendance, ratios, revenue, staff hours, enrollment trends, subsidy vs private pay
